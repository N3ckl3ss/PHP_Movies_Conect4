Menu

You can choose two different playing mods(on and two player), If you click start thegame will begin. If you want to change the player names you must click save name befor continuing.

Gameplay

Objective of the Game

To be the first player to Make 4 same color checkers in a row diagonally, horizontally or vertically.

Connect 4 Rule

Each turn, the player will get the chance to drop and make the row of his checkers, but sometimes instead of making your row, the player has to stop another opponent from making his 4 checkers row by dropping your color checker in that place. The game ends when any players make four checkers of his color in a row, or when all the 42 slots are occupied, in that case, the game ends in a tie

Controls

You can play this game with a mouse or a keybord. You can click the columns or press the number(1-7) to place the coin
